1. Tosa is the historical name for Kochi Prefecture, where Kochi City is located.
2. Mimase is a town, and makes a pun with the similar-sounding word following
3. Urado is another town, and sounds like "back door"...another pun
4. Katsuobushi is a seasoning made of dried tuna.
5. This section, per World Folk Song, is a bit of local pride boasting.
"Kochi can't be beat - no matter what you say, there are whales in our pond!"
6. The "pond" here being the ocean.
7. Literally "squirting fish", so likely whales or dolphins.

What are the rules of participation?

For a team to participate in a yosakoi festival, there are certain rules that must be followed. These rules may vary from festival to festival, but as the Kochi festival is the original, it is likely that these rules are included in other festivals as well. Per the official Kochi Yosakoi Matsuri website:

  • The choreography must include holding naruko (clackers) in each hand.
  • The music can be whatever you wish, but must incorporate the original Yosakoi Naruko Odori music in some way.
  • Dancers are limited to 150 per team.
  • If using a vehicle, it must be less than 9m (29.5ft) in length and 3.6m(11.8ft) in height, including any attached signs or other decorations.
